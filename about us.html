About Us

At M-Pox Connect, we are committed to raising awareness and providing resources about M-Pox. Our team is dedicated to delivering accurate information, supporting communities, and promoting health education.

Our Vision

To create a world where communities are well-informed, proactive, and empowered to prevent and manage M-Pox effectively.

Our Mission

To educate and support individuals by providing clear, research-backed information about M-Pox, its prevention, and its effects on public health.

Our History

M-Pox Connect was founded in 2025 in response to the increasing spread of M-Pox. Recognizing the lack of awareness and misinformation, we assembled a team of researchers, medical experts, and community leaders to bridge the gap in knowledge.
